  • {{1234|currency}}
  • {{1234|currency:'@'}}
  • {{1234.6789|currency:'RMB'}}

date日期过滤器

  • {{today}}
  • {{today|date:'medium'}}
  • {{today|date:'yyyy'}}
  • {{today|date:'yy'}}
  • {{today|date:'y'}}
  • 英文月份：{{today|date:'MMMM'}}
  • 英文月份简写：{{today|date:'MMM'}}
  • 数字月份：{{today|date:'MM'}}
  • 一年中第几个月份：{{today|date:'M'}}
  • {{today|date:'yyyy年MM月dd HH:mm:ss'}}
  • {{today|date:'yyyy/MM/dd HH:mm:ss'}}
